Foreword

It's been thirteen years of Dillo project, and 40 releases along the way!

We didn't succeed in the effort to fund the core developers to work full-time on it. Now we're trying a different strategy, and have had some success starting with partnering.

A web browser is too complex to be undertaken on a spare time basis, so we need your help and support.

After three years of trying, the funding initiative didn't yield any positive results... So we're still asking our users for support.

Did you know that the FSF receives 75% of its income from individual donors?

You can make the difference.
